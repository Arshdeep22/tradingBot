1. STRATEGY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This strategy identifies Supply and Demand zones on price charts and takes 
trades when price returns to these zones. The key principle is that institutional 
orders leave "footprints" in the form of supply/demand zones, and price often 
returns to these zones to fill remaining orders.

- Trade Type: Intraday / Swing
- Risk:Reward: 1:3
- Timeframes: 15m (primary), 5m, 3m (confirmation)
- Markets: Indian Stocks (NSE)

================================================================================
2. ZONE DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

DEMAND ZONE (Buy Zone):
-----------------------
A demand zone is formed when price drops, consolidates briefly (base), 
and then rallies sharply upward (leg out).

Pattern: DROP → BASE (1-5 candles) → RALLY (leg out)

- The BASE area becomes the Demand Zone
- Zone Top = Highest high of base candles
- Zone Bottom = Lowest low of base candles
- We BUY when price returns to this zone

SUPPLY ZONE (Sell Zone):
-------------------------
A supply zone is formed when price rallies, consolidates briefly (base), 
and then drops sharply downward (leg out).

Pattern: RALLY → BASE (1-5 candles) → DROP (leg out)

- The BASE area becomes the Supply Zone
- Zone Top = Highest high of base candles
- Zone Bottom = Lowest low of base candles
- We SELL/SHORT when price returns to this zone

3. ZONE SCORING SYSTEM (Total: 100 points)
================================================================================

A zone must score >= 80 points to be considered tradeable.

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ CRITERIA 1: FRESHNESS (Max 40 points)                                       │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                             │
│ A FRESH zone is one that has NEVER been tested (price has not returned      │
│ to the zone since it was formed).                                           │
│                                                                             │
│ Scoring:                                                                    │
│   - Zone never tested (completely fresh)    = 40 points                     │
│   - Zone tested once (partially used)       = 0 points (DISCARD)            │
│   - Zone tested multiple times              = 0 points (DISCARD)            │
│                                                                             │
│ Note: Once a zone is tested, it is NO LONGER VALID. Remove it.             │
│                                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ CRITERIA 2: LEG-OUT STRENGTH (Max 30 points)                                │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                             │
│ The leg-out candle is the candle that LEAVES the zone. A bigger, more       │
│ explosive candle = stronger zone = more institutional interest.              │
│                                                                             │
│ Measurement: Body size of leg-out candle as % of price                      │
│                                                                             │
│ Scoring:                                                                    │
│   - STRONGEST (Exciting): Body >= 1.5% of price     = 30 points            │
│   - STRONGER: Body between 1.0% - 1.5% of price    = 20 points            │
│   - STRONG: Body between 0.5% - 1.0% of price      = 10 points            │
│   - WEAK: Body < 0.5% of price                     = 0 points (DISCARD)   │
│                                                                             │
│ Additional factors (bonus consideration):                                   │
│   - Candle closes near its high/low (full body, tiny wicks) = better       │
│   - Multiple large candles leaving zone = even better                       │
│   - Gap away from zone = strongest signal                                   │
│                                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ CRITERIA 3: BASE CANDLES (Max 30 points)                                    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                             │
│ The base is the consolidation area that forms the zone. Fewer candles       │
│ in the base = more explosive move = stronger zone.                          │
│                                                                             │
│ Scoring:                                                                    │
│   - 1-2 base candles (tightest base)       = 30 points                     │
│   - 3-4 base candles                       = 20 points                     │
│   - 5 base candles                         = 10 points                     │
│   - More than 5 base candles               = 0 points (DISCARD)            │
│                                                                             │
│ Note: Base candles are typically small-bodied (doji, spinning top,          │
│ inside bars). They show indecision before the explosive move.               │
│                                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

TOTAL SCORE = Freshness + Leg-out Strength + Base Candles

TRADE FILTER: Only take trades with score >= 80

================================================================================
4. ENTRY RULES
================================================================================

DEMAND ZONE (Long/Buy):
- Entry:  At the TOP of the demand zone (zone_top)
- Reason: When price returns to zone top, it's the first touch

SUPPLY ZONE (Short/Sell):
- Entry:  At the BOTTOM of the supply zone (zone_bottom)
- Reason: When price returns to zone bottom, it's the first touch

================================================================================
5. STOP LOSS RULES
================================================================================

DEMAND ZONE (Long/Buy):
- Stop Loss: Below the BOTTOM of the demand zone
- Buffer: Add 0.1% buffer below zone bottom for safety
- Formula: SL = zone_bottom * (1 - 0.001)

SUPPLY ZONE (Short/Sell):
- Stop Loss: Above the TOP of the supply zone
- Buffer: Add 0.1% buffer above zone top for safety
- Formula: SL = zone_top * (1 + 0.001)

================================================================================
6. TARGET RULES
================================================================================

Risk:Reward Ratio = 1:3

DEMAND ZONE (Long/Buy):
- Risk = Entry - Stop Loss
- Target = Entry + (3 × Risk)
- Formula: Target = entry + 3 * (entry - stop_loss)

SUPPLY ZONE (Short/Sell):
- Risk = Stop Loss - Entry
- Target = Entry - (3 × Risk)
- Formula: Target = entry - 3 * (stop_loss - entry)

================================================================================
7. ZONE DETECTION ALGORITHM
================================================================================

Step 1: Identify potential zones
  - Look for sharp moves (leg-in or leg-out candles > 0.5% body)
  - Find consolidation (base) before the sharp move out

Step 2: Define zone boundaries
  - Zone Top = max(high of all base candles)
  - Zone Bottom = min(low of all base candles)

Step 3: Check freshness
  - After zone forms, check if price has returned to the zone
  - If price touches or enters the zone = TESTED (discard)
  - If price never returned = FRESH (valid)

Step 4: Score the zone
  - Apply scoring criteria (freshness + leg-out + base)
  - Only keep zones with score >= 80

Step 5: Calculate trade levels
  - Entry, Stop Loss, Target based on rules above

================================================================================
8. POSITION SIZING
================================================================================

- Maximum risk per trade: 1% of capital
- Position size = Risk Amount / (Entry - Stop Loss)
- Example: Capital = 100,000, Risk = 1,000
  If Entry = 1000, SL = 990 → Risk per share = 10
  Position size = 1000 / 10 = 100 shares

================================================================================
9. TRADE MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

- Take 1-2 trades per day MAXIMUM (best zones only)
- If multiple zones score 80+, take the HIGHEST scoring one
- Do NOT add to losing positions
- Do NOT move stop loss further away
- Can trail stop loss to breakeven after 1:1 is achieved (optional)
- Exit at target (1:3) or stop loss - NO exceptions

================================================================================
10. FILTERS & ADDITIONAL RULES
================================================================================

DO NOT TRADE:
- First 15 minutes after market open (9:15 - 9:30 AM)
- Last 15 minutes before market close (3:15 - 3:30 PM)
- When major news/events are expected
- If the broader market (Nifty) is very volatile (> 2% move)

ADDITIONAL ZONE QUALITY CHECKS:
- Zone should be on a clear trend (demand in uptrend, supply in downtrend)
- Multiple timeframe confirmation preferred (zone on 15m, entry on 5m)
- Avoid zones too close to each other (within 0.5%)
